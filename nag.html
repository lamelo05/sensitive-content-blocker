Action Required

Allow In Incognito

The Sensitive Content Blocker is currently unable to protect your private browsing sessions because all web browsers aggressively disable extensions in Incognito Mode by default.

How to Fix This:

  1. Open your browser's extensions manager page. (Example for Chrome: type chrome://extensions in the URL bar)
  2. Find the Sensitive Content Blocker box and click the Details button.
  3. Scroll down through the settings and toggle Allow in Incognito to the ON position.
  4. You will now be completely protected in private windows! Feel free to close this tab.
